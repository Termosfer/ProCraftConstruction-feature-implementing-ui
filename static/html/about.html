About Us

Established in 2018, PROCRAFT Limited Liability Company is an innovative Engineering Company based in Baku, Azerbaijan. With the experienced professional engineering staff, PROCRAFT is targeting to provide world-class Engineering, Construction, Management, and Inspection services in the local and regional market. At PROCRAFT, we foster a culture of creativity, innovation, collaboration, integrity, and accountability. We believe in empowering our team members, fostering a supportive and inclusive work environment, promoting continuous learning, professional growth, and personal development, and celebrating achievements, milestones, and successes together as a team.
